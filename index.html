In everyone's life, at some time, our inner fire goes out. It is then burst into flame by an encounter with another human being. We should all be thankful for those people who rekindle the inner spirit. When it comes to me, I have one friend to specially thank.

She can be silent with me in a moment of despair or confusion, she can stay with me in an hour of grief and bereavement, she can tolerate not knowing... not healing, not curing... she is a friend who really cares - Anjali. No, it doesn't mean "five rats" as we mallus have made up. It means "Offering" in Sanskrit and "Angel" in Latin.

She is a friend of mine. She gather me, man. The pieces I am, she gather them and give them back to me in all the right order. The best part about being with Anjali is that I can afford to be stupid. She adores you even though she know the things you're most ashamed of. There is a magnet in her heart that attract true friends. That magnet is unselfishness, thinking of others first. And when you learn to live for others, they will live for you. Thank you Anjali, For being my friend


- Jyothis Joy
